What is Adventure On?

Adventure On is an innovative travel booking platform that transforms your social media travel inspiration videos into real trips.

How does it work?

Simply save videos of experiences, destinations, or activities you love from Instagram and TikTok—on your iPhone, while watching the video, tap the share button (the square with an upward arrow on Instagram, or the paper airplane on TikTok). This opens a menu at the bottom of the screen showing apps and options (often called the share menu or share options). Just scroll or swipe through it and tap Adventure On to send the video directly to the app. Do a quick save for later or hit plan trip to discover more about your inspiration instantly.

Where can I save travel inspiration from?

Currently, it is only possible to save video inspiration from Instagram & Tiktok. More platforms will be enabled in 2026.

Is Adventure On available for both iOS and Android devices?

It is only available as an app on IOS. A desktop and Android version is currently being developed.

What makes Adventure On better than the other apps?

One app does it all: Discover from your clips → get tailored itineraries → book flights, hotels, and activities — no jumping between Google Flights, Booking.com, TripIt, etc.
